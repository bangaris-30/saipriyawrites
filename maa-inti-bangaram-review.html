Who Needs SuperWomen When We Have The Samantha

That was the first thought that popped into my head when I was watching Swarna (Samantha) beating goons to pulp, gun in the hand so elegant as a pen is to a writer.

The story follows Swarna and her husband going to their family after years of abandonment on the occasion of his sister's marriage. What follows is how Swarna tries to fit in the role of daughter-in-law so that their family could forgive and accept their love match.

In between this pursuit, she comes face to face with her unusual past. The rest of the story tries to balance how she gives a new definition to the perfect daughter-in-law, aka Maa Inti Bangaram, when they face different hurdles and she becomes the battle shield.

This cinema stands out in perfectly mixing the humour, fight and the Telugu touch in Jargaon. A special mention to how Samantha shines in her sarees perfectly across the action scenes, creating for us a real Indian SuperWoman.

Nandini Reddy once again proves how well she can make a complete entertainer while still holding the story.

Definitely a mood uplifter and Must Watch.

★★★★☆
